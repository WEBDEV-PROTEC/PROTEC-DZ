Odoo Website Stock Notify

Notify your Customers for stock availability in Odoo Website

Odoo Website Stock Notify module helps you to send e-mail notification to their customers regarding the product availability. Customers can opt to receive email notification when the product is in the stock.

Stock management is one of the important parts for selling any product for offline as well as online shops. Hence, letting your customers know the status of the stock is really crucial; as it builds a strong relation of trust with them.

Now, you can keep your customers updated with the stock of your products with simple notification. Odoo Website Stock Notify allows you to send notification via mail to your customers as per the availability of the products.

Notify Your customers for the Stock Availability in Odoo Website

Now, you can facilitate your customers to get the notification whenever the product gets stocked in your Odoo Website. Your customers can opt whether they want to receive the notification or not.
The customers can add their valid email id so that email is sent to them without any complication.
Basically, when a customers see the product out of stock then they can ask to get notification whenever the product stock if fulfilled.

Create e-Mail Templates for the Stock Availability Notification

The module facilitates you to create the templates for the email notifications which are sent to the users. You can set the content of the mail as you want.
If you wish for the mail to be automatically sent to the customer when the product is in stock.
You can set the cron scheduler for the mail notification as you want so that your customers do not feel any kind of inconvenience.

Notify Your customers for the Stock Availability in Odoo Website

  • It helps the Odoo admin to notify the customers about the availability of an out-of-stock product on the website.
  • The customers can opt to provide their email address in the ‘Stock Notification’ tab on the product page to receive mail notification when the product is back in stock.
  • The admin can choose to send manually or automatically in the Odoo.
  • Automatic mail notifications are sent with the help of Cron scheduler.
  • The Odoo admin can configure the Cron scheduler as per the requirements.
  • The standard mail template can be set in the Odoo.
