What Is Product Slot Pricing?

Product Slot Pricing is a technique of setting the different prices according to discounts, offers, and quantities. By offering different slot prices, you offer customers the ease of choosing the best option available.

Odoo Website Product Slot Pricing adds numerous pricing slots on the product page. And let your customers choose the best option for them. The module allows you to create more than one slot (offer) price and add the time and quantity of the product.

Significance Of Product Slot Pricing

Slot pricing can vary from product to product and can change day by day. Each product slot is unique and offers different prices for the same product.

Apart from that, customers can buy a single quantity of a product at a regular price. In simple words, slot pricing offers different price options to the customers. It allows the customers to choose the best option to buy the product.

Benefits Of Product Slot Pricing For Your Business

Product slot pricing offers various benefits for your business:

  • Slot pricing enables you to present a different price option to your customers.
  • It helps you sell multiple quantities of products effortlessly.
  • Provide opportunities to create loyal and long-term customers.
  • It helps increase product sales and increase conversion.
  • You can change prices daily to know the customer purchasing pattern and behavior that will let you offer good service to them.
